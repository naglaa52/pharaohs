PHARAOHS

In this project AI has reconstructed the faces of long dead Egyptian kings and queens, using only their statues. Also a simulated video with AI-modified images and graphics is made to tell about the character by turning text of pharaoh's life into videos with AI voices. We have also added the most famous landmarks for these personalities. We provide the 3D view of the pharaoh's statues and making it interactive also we provide the 360 degree photo of some of these landmarks. to show people how ancient Egypt was like and how Pharaohs looked like in a modern and simple way that will attract user attention without feeling bored . we also Provided locations of landmarks and historical places where visitors can find Pharaohs temples where their statues are and Information about gods and goddesses in that era and how was the religious life back then. The team is constantly working to update and improve the website based on user feedback, ensuring that it remains a valuable resource for anyone interested in the fascinating world of the Pharaohs. They are highly professional and dedicated, with a deep understanding of the project's requirements and expectations.
