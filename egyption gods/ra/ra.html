ra

God of the Sun

"The sun will shine in your life once again"

Ra was considered the ruler of the sky and the earth, and his daily journey across the sky in his solar bark was seen as a representation of the cycle of life, death and rebirth. The Egyptians believed that Ra's journey through the underworld during the night was a time of judgment, where the souls of the deceased were judged by the god Anubis, and the righteous were allowed to join Ra in his journey through the sky. Overall, Ra was considered the creator of the world, the ruler of the sky and the earth and one of the most powerful and important gods
